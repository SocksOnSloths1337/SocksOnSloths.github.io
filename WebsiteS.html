How to Buy SLOTH Token

  1. Step 1: Open an account on a cryptocurrency exchange such as Binance or Coinbase.
  2. Step 2: Deposit funds into your exchange account using your preferred payment method.
  3. Step 3: Download TrustWallet and transfer BNB to your wallet.
  4. Step 4: Convert BNB to BNB Smart Chain.
  5. Step 5: In your TrustWallet, scroll over to 'Browser' and search for PancakeSwap.
  6. Step 6: Search for SLOTH token using its contract address or symbol (SLOTH).
  7. Step 7: Place a buy order for SLOTH token using BNB Smart Chain, specifying the amount you wish to purchase.
  8. Step 8: Once your order is filled, you can withdraw the purchased SLOTH tokens to your personal wallet.
  9. Step 9: If you do not see your tokens in your wallet, you will have to manually import them using the SLOTH wallet address. To do this, go to the home page of TrustWallet, click on the 'Tokens' button at the top right of the screen, click the '+' symbol, choose the BNB Smart Chain Network, and paste in the SLOTH coin address.
